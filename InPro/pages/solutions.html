Supporting Services

Specialized capabilities for complex files

RCV / ACV Valuation

Replacement Cost Value and Actual Cash Value calculated per line item using structured depreciation methodology. Canada-first LKQ sourcing — major retailers first, specialty vendors second, allowance-based third. eBay and secondhand markets are last resort only.

Complex & High-Value Item Analysis

Fine art, jewellery, watches, collectibles, musical instruments, electronics, and specialty items require dedicated research beyond standard retail sourcing. InPro applies specialized sourcing methodology and supporting documentation for each high-value item.

Catastrophe Claim Support

Large-scale events require consistent methodology at volume. InPro provides scalable catastrophe claim support with the same output standard applied to every file — regardless of volume or timeline pressure. Ideal for carriers managing post-CAT surge.

Dispute & Appraisal Support

When a contents claim becomes contested, the quality of the underlying documentation determines the outcome. InPro provides litigation-ready Schedule of Loss analysis, expert engagement for formal appraisal proceedings, and technical support for legal counsel and carriers.
